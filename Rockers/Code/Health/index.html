Frequently Asked Questions

01

How accurate is the symptom checker?

Our symptom checker is designed as a preliminary guide and not a replacement for professional medical advice. It uses data from trusted medical sources but should be used as a starting point for discussions with healthcare providers.

02

Can I create a personalized diet plan?

Yes, our diet planning tool allows you to input your health goals, dietary restrictions, and preferences to generate a customized nutrition plan. You can further adjust it to suit your specific needs.

03

How often is the health articles section updated?

We update our articles section weekly with new content from health experts and researchers. All information is reviewed for accuracy and relevance before publication.

04

Are the health calculators suitable for everyone?

Our calculators are designed for adults and provide general guidance. They may not be suitable for pregnant women, professional athletes, or individuals with certain medical conditions. Always consult with a healthcare provider for personalized advice.
