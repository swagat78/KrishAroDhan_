Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator

BMI is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or greater

Heart Rate Zones Calculator

Calculate your target heart rate zones for optimal exercise intensity based on your age and fitness level.

Heart Rate Zones:

  • Zone 1 (50-60%): Very light intensity, warm-up/recovery
  • Zone 2 (60-70%): Light intensity, improves basic endurance
  • Zone 3 (70-80%): Moderate intensity, improves aerobic fitness
  • Zone 4 (80-90%): Hard intensity, improves anaerobic threshold
  • Zone 5 (90-100%): Maximum intensity, increases maximum performance

Measurement Instructions:

  • Neck: Measure around the middle of your neck, below the larynx (Adam's apple).
  • Waist: Measure at the narrowest point, usually around the navel.
  • Hip: Measure at the widest point around the buttocks.

Use a flexible tape measure and ensure it's snug but not tight.
